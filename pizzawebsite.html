Pizza Restaurant Website

A recreation of a real business website, adding support for mobile devices, improving navigation, load times, and more.

Description

Villa Pizza is a local pizza restaurant with an outdated website.

I saw opportunities for improvement based on the business' needs and created this.

Landing Page With Call To Action

The original website's landing page had far too many things drawing the user's attention, with most of it being unnecessary. I created a simpler design.

Mobile-Friendly Design

Since over 55% of website traffic comes from mobile devices, I ensured the website was compatible accross all devices to avoid losing customers.

Organized and Responsive Menu

The outdated menu is built accross different webpages, which creates difficulties in loadtimes, navigation, and design consistency. I consolidated the whole menu in one page, which can easily be navigated in submenus and is also mobile-friendly.
